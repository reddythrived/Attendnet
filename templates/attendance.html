Live camera-based attendance

Start Face Attendance

Keep your face in front of the camera for at least 20 seconds. If your attendance is already marked for today, the system will show "Reg_No already marked" in the console.

Step 1
Run smart_face_attendance.py
python smart_face_attendance.py
Step 2
Stand alone in front of the camera.
Wait until your USN stays green.

Note: This button is only a visual hint. You actually start the camera from your Python script.

Excel sheet: Attendance is saved with today’s date as a new column. All other students remain Absent unless the camera sees and confirms them.
